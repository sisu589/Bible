And from thence I went towards the north to the ends of the earth, and there I saw a great and glorious device at the ends of the whole earth.
And here I saw three portals of heaven open in the heaven: through each o them proceed north winds: when they low there is cold, hail, frost, snow, dew, and rain.
And out of one portal they blow for good: but when they blow through the other two portals, it is with violence and affliciton on the earth, and they blow with violence.
