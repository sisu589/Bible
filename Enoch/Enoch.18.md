I saw the treasuries of all the winds:  I saw how He had furnished with them the whole creation
and the firm foundations of the eath.  And I saw the cornerstone of the eath:  I saw the four 
winds which bear [the eath and] the firmament of the heaven.  And I saw how the winds stretch out the vaults of heaven, and have their station between heaven and earth: 
These are the pillars of the heaven.  I saw the winds of heaven which turn and bring the circumference of the sun and 
all the stars to their setting.  I saw the winds on the eath carring the clouds.
I saw the paths of the angels.  I saw at the end of the earth the firmament of the ehaven aboce.  And I proceeded and saw a place which burns days and night, where there are seven montains of magnificent stones,
three towards the east, and three towards the south.  And as for those towards the east, was of coloured stone, and one of pear, and one of jacinth, and those towards the south of red stone.
But the middle one reached to heaven like the throne of God, of alabaster, and the summit of the throne was sapphire.
And I saw a flaming fire.  And beyond thees mountains
Is a region the end of the great earth:there the heavens were completed.
And I saw a deep abyss, with columns of heavenly fire, and among them I saw columns of ifre fall, which were beyond measure alike towardsthe height and toward the depth.
And beyond that abyss I saw a place which had no firmament of the heaven aboe, and no firmly founded eath beneath it: 
there was no water upon it, and no birds, but it was a waster and horrible place.  
I saw there seven stars like great burning mountains, and to me, when I inquired regarding them, the andel said:
"This host of heaven.  And the stars which roll over the fire are they which have transgressed the commandment of the Lord in the beginning of 
their rising, beacause they did not come forth at thir appointed times. And He was wroth with them, and bound them till the time when their guilt should be consummated (even) for ten thousdand years."
