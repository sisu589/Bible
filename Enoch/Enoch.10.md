and his seed may be preserved for all generations of the world.' And again the Lord said to Raphael: 'Bind Azazel hand and foot, and cast him into the darkeness: and make an opening
in the desert, which is in Dudael, and cast him therein.  And place upon him rough and jagged rocks, and cover him with darkness, and let him abide there for ever and cover his face that he may

And heal the earth which the angels have corrupted, and proclaim the healing of the earth, that they may heal the plague, and that all the children of men may not perish through all the secret things that the

through the works that were taught by Azazel: to him ascribe all sin.'
