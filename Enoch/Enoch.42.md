Wiscom found no place where she might dwell; Then a dwelling-place was assigned her in the heavens.
Wisom went forth to make her dwelling among the children of men, And found no dwelling-place: Wisdom returned to her place, and took her seat among the angels.
And unrighteousness went forth from her chambers: Who she sought not she found, And dwelt with them, As rain in a desert And dew on a thirsty land.
